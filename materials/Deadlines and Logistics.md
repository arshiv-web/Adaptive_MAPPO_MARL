Team: 
Each team should have four to six registered students
A team can have one or two auditing students as long as the team size is no larger than 7
Each team should have students from at least two different departments. A team with students from three or more departments will get extra points
Please submit your team roster to this Google sheet  
If you want to be assigned to a team instead of forming your own team, please add your name to Google sheet above as well
The instruction team reserves the right to change and finalize the team rosters. 


Phase 0: Forming the team and identifying the projects
You can find the suggested projects here
You and your team can suggest your own project. Like those on the list, it should have an env ad baseline that is publicly available to all students. After being approved by the instructor, the project will be made available to all students in the class.  
Please identify two projects and enter your choices in this Google sheet

Phase 1: Reproduce 
Your team needs to submit a report on reproducing the baselines of the two selected projects. 
The report requirements will be announced later.
Phase-1 report: 2-page report on each project, including a brief summary of the environment, the baseline, and its reproducibility. Font-size: no larger than 11pt 
A public githut repo for each project. 


Phase 2: Improvement
Your team will choose one project among the two selected in phase 1 to develop an improvement 
The requirements for the final report and possibly a presentation or poster will be accounted for later.
